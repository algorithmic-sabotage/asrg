    Police Officers Faces (POF) A large-scale face recognition dataset containing of approximately 90,000 headshot photos of thousands of police officers. This dataset is intended as a tool to empower community members engaged in copwatch and other counter-surveillance practices. Posted: Jun 17, 2024.
