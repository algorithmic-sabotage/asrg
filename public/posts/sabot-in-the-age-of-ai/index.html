    Context #

    The list titled Sabot in the Age of AI catalogues strategically offensive methodologies and purposefully orchestrated tactics intended to facilitate (algorithmic) sabotage, including the deliberate disruption of structures and processes, as well as the targeted poisoning and corruption of data within the operational workflows of artificial intelligence (AI) systems. Each approach delineated herein has been meticulously designed to systematically subvert the integrity of training pipelines, derail data acquisition procedures, and fundamentally undermine the foundational pillars that uphold the efficacy, reliability, and functionality of AI-driven frameworks.
